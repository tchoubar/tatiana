</h2>
</div>
<div class="art-PostHeaderIcons art-metadata-icons">
December 13th, 2012
</div>
<div class="art-PostContent">
<p>Well, I&#8217;m sitting here trying to figure out what to write for my second WordPress post. My 15 year old son just came blasting through the door, home from school. Now he wants to friggin&#8217; chat. I just want to get this done! Now, I have to listen to him whine and complain how hard his day was.</p>
<p>Right now, he has the largest spoon we own, extracting what appears to be about 1 pound of peanut butter from the jar!</p>
<p>God, I need a vacation!</p>
